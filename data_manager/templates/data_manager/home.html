Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my personal information?

We use enterprise-grade encryption and security protocols to protect all user data. Your privacy and anonymity options are fully supported, and we comply with all data protection regulations.

Can I report incidents anonymously?

Yes, ICARE supports anonymous reporting. You can choose to provide contact information for follow-up or submit completely anonymous reports based on your comfort level.

How long does it take to get a response?

Emergency incidents receive immediate attention within minutes. Non-emergency reports typically receive acknowledgment within 24 hours, with investigation updates provided throughout the process.

What types of incidents can I report?

You can report crimes, suspicious activities, community safety concerns, infrastructure issues, and other incidents affecting public safety and community wellbeing.
